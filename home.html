About myGGMC.com

This password-protected site is for the use of the members of GGMC to quickly locate useful files and contact information for the officers in the chorus. If you have any questions, suggestions, or comments, please contact Ben Vaughn.

Donate to GGMC

Our Mission

Representing LGBTQ+ individuals, we strive to build community, foster compassion, and inspire activism at home and around the world through our music and message.


Chorus Values

Our Vision: We envision a world inspired and unified by the music we create.

Transforming Unity

We believe in the transformative power of music to heal, to enlighten and to foster unity.

Life-Affirming All

We embrace the life-affirming changes we create within and beyond our chorus community.

Changing Hearts

We evolve society’s views toward LGBTQ+ individuals through our commitment to excellence.

Honoring Our Past

We honor all who came before us whose sacrifices made it possible for us to raise our voices.
